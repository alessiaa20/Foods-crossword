Pronunciation Tips

  • ă = "uh" (like sofa)
  • â / î = deep "i" from throat
  • ș = "sh"
  • ț = "ts"

Easy Sentence-Building Tips

  • Tip 1: Subject + Verb + Object
    Ex: Eu voi mânca pâine.
  • Tip 2: Start with a template
    Ex: "I will [verb] [food]."
  • Tip 3: Use words you know
  • Tip 4: Use familiar starters:
    Ce...? Vrei...? Îmi place... Vom merge...
Tu vei bea vin.
